## RESPONSE GENERATION

Ooh, building on the time-travel idea:

**"Undo as Archaeology"**
What if reverting changes felt like an archaeological dig? Each undo takes you down a layer, and you can see the "strata" of all your past decisions. Brush away the surface to reveal what was underneath.

**"Collaborative Ghosts"**
When you're editing, you see translucent "ghosts" of where other users are working in real-time. Not intrusive - just ambient awareness. Like seeing footprints in snow.

**"Data as Music"**
Different data patterns make different sounds. Anomalies create dissonance. When your system is healthy, it hums in harmony. You could literally "hear" when something breaks.

**"Metaphor Skins"**
Switch between completely different interface metaphors: forest (data as trees), ocean (data as currents), city (data as buildings). Same functionality, totally different mental models.

The interpretive dance one is wild - but what if gestures controlled *data transformations*? Swipe to filter, spiral to aggregate, expand arms to zoom out...
